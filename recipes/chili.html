Chili

Home

Chili is a delicious comfort food made mostly with canned goods, and is relatively easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 28 oz crushed tomatoes
  • 15 oz kidney beans
  • 15 oz chili beans
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 medium onion
  • Chili powder
  • Oregano
  • Cumin
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Sour cream

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef over medium high heat. Drain any excess fat.
  2. Add garlic and onions. Cook until garlic is fragrent and onions are transluscent.
  3. Add crushed tomatoes, beans, and spices. Simmer for 20-30 minutes.
  4. Serve with cheddar cheese and sour cream on top.
